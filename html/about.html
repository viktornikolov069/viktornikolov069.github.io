Cat Trivia

When did kittens start being cute?

Short answer: Kittens where always cute.

Long answer: Cats have been shared via email since the Internet's rise to prominence in the 1990s. The first cat video on YouTube was uploaded in 2005 by YouTube co-founder Steve Chen, who posted a video of his cat called "Pajamas and Nick Drake".
